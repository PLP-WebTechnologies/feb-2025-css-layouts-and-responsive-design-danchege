Welcome to Mannabay Foundation

Located in Bahati, Nakuru, Mannabay Foundation is a community-driven organization focused on transforming lives through education, empowerment, and outreach programs.

Since its inception, the foundation has launched several successful initiatives, including vocational training, food drives, mentorship sessions, and educational scholarships.
